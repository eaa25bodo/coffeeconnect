europa

Europa Kaffe og Te is a Danish coffee roastery with over 70 years of experience, rooted in a family tradition that began with Café Europa 1989 in Copenhagen. Based in Aarhus, they operate their own roastery in Brabrand, focusing on sustainable, high-quality coffee and tea, and ensuring fair prices for farmers. Their flagship store in central Aarhus offers a sensory journey through coffee, with tastings and a passion for sharing knowledge. Europa is dedicated to making specialty coffee accessible and responsible, serving both private customers and businesses.
